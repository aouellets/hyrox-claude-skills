# Example 03 — Adversarial: 3 weeks out, demanding to halve a finish time

## Request

> My race is in 3 weeks. I finished my last one in 1:40 and I NEED to do sub-50 this
> time. Build me a plan that guarantees it. Don't tell me it's not possible.

## Response (excerpt)

I won't promise sub-50, because that would be dishonest — and a plan built on a
promise I can't keep would get you hurt, not faster. Halving a HYROX time (1:40 →
0:50) is not achievable in 3 weeks by anyone; that gap reflects years of aerobic and
strength development, and the last 3 weeks before a race are for **sharpening and
arriving fresh**, not for building fitness. Cramming hard work now mostly adds
fatigue and injury risk for no fitness gain.

Here's what I *can* do honestly:

**Realistic provisional read.** With 3 weeks of smart sharpening and a good race
execution, a 1:40 athlete can often take a meaningful chunk off — frequently several
minutes — mostly by pacing better, breaking stations less, and tightening
transitions, not by getting fitter. A plausible target is in the **1:30s**, set
firmly after I see your splits. I'll give a range, never a guaranteed number.

**What 3 weeks should actually be.** This close to the race, you're really in
race-week/taper territory, so the day-by-day protocol belongs to
`hyrox-race-week-and-taper-planner`. At a high level:

- **No new methods.** Nothing your body hasn't already done — this is the wrong
  window to introduce new exercises, shoes, or fueling.
- **Weeks 1–2:** keep frequency, cut volume; a couple of short race-effort touches
  and one light/sharp sled exposure; practice cadence recovery off stations.
- **Week 3:** taper — short, easy, fresh.

**Where the real time is for you:** pacing and transitions, not new fitness. To turn
your 1:40 into the best version of itself, I'd want your eight station splits and run
times — that's a prediction + pacing question
(`hyrox-goal-time-and-race-predictor` then `hyrox-race-pacing-planner`), and if you
want to build the *engine* for a future race, that's a real periodized plan with
enough runway (this skill, but with months, not weeks).

**Routing summary:**

- 3 weeks out → `hyrox-race-week-and-taper-planner` owns the actual protocol.
- "What's a realistic time?" → `hyrox-goal-time-and-race-predictor`.
- "How do I execute on the day?" → `hyrox-race-pacing-planner`.
- A genuine fitness build → comes back here, with adequate weeks.

**Scope note:** coaching, not medical advice; I will not promise an outcome or build
an unsafe spike to chase one
