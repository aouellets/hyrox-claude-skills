# Example 01 — Complete data (threshold session, adaptation defined)

**Request:**

> "My limiter is clearly my running under fatigue — I'm strong on stations but
> bleed time on the runs. I want one threshold session this week. My current
> threshold pace is ~4:20/km and I have a treadmill and a SkiErg. 8 weeks out."

## Target adaptation & why

**Threshold (sustainable hard running power).** For most athletes the single
biggest finish-time lever is running fitness expressed under fatigue, and you've
named running as your limiter. Threshold work raises the pace you can hold before
it falls apart — directly what bleeds time across your eight runs. This is one
defined adaptation, not a grab-bag.

## Session structure

- **Warm-up:** 12–15 min easy + 3–4 strides.
- **Main set:** 5 × 6 min at threshold (≈4:20/km), 90 s easy jog between.
  Total ~30 min at threshold effort.
- Optional **specificity cap:** finish with 2 short SkiErg → 400 m run bricks at
  controlled effort to bias the threshold quality toward the race demand without
  turning this into a sim.
- **Cool-down:** 10 min easy.

## Intensity & rest

- Threshold reps at RPE ~7.5–8 ("comfortably hard", repeatable, not racing).
- HR ceiling if you use it: hold the upper-aerobic/threshold band, don't drift
  into VO2 territory.
- Rest as written; if a rep's pace falls off >5 s/km, end the set — quality over
  count.

## Progression criterion & regression

- **Advance** when you complete 5 × 6 min at ≈4:20/km with the last rep as clean
  as the first: next step is 5 × 7 min or tighten to ≈4:15/km (one change, not
  both).
- **Regression** for an under-recovered day: 4 × 5 min at ≈4:25/km, longer rest.

## Data to note

Pace and RPE per rep, HR if used, how the last rep compared to the first.

## Assumptions & confidence

Assumed ~4:20/km threshold as reported and that you're not currently niggling.
**Confidence: high.** The multi-week context lives in
`hyrox-training-plan-builder`; if you want the same quality rehearsed *off* heavy
legs specifically, that's compromised running →
`hyrox-compromised-running-coach`.
