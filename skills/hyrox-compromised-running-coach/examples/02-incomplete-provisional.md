# Example 02 — Incomplete data: which station fades worst is unknown

## Request

> My runs fall apart later in HYROX races and I lose loads of time. Help me train
> running when I'm tired. That's all I really know.

## Response (provisional — labeled)

**This is a provisional session set.** You've told me the runs fade, but not
*which stations* cause the worst fade or any compromised-pace numbers, so I'll
default to the highest-cost transitions and flag where the priority shifts once
you confirm.

**What I'm missing and why it matters:**

| Gap | Effect |
|---|---|
| Which stations wreck the next run most | Decides which bricks get the most reps. I'm defaulting to sled-to-run and lunge-to-run (usually the worst), but yours may differ. |
| A compromised pace reference (or fresh pace) | Sets the first-200 m and settle targets. Without it, today's work is by-RPE and a measured baseline. |

**Default focus (until you confirm):** sled-to-run and lunge-to-run — these cost
the most for most athletes, so they're the safest default.

**Baseline brick (do this first, by RPE, and report the numbers):**
- Sled push ~15 m at a controlled hard effort → 400 m run at "compromised
  comfortably-hard" (RPE ~8), noting your average pace and how long until your
  turnover feels normal. That gives me your real targets.

**Provisional transition drill (works regardless of paces):**
1. Pace control — first 200 m a touch easy, don't chase fresh pace off heavy legs.
2. Cadence — short, light, quick steps to rebuild turnover first.
3. Breathing — imposed exhale-focused rhythm.
4. Posture — ribs down, eyes up, run tall.

**Provisional structure:** 2 brick sessions/week, 3–4 sled-to-run reps each, start
conservative. Not a full simulation — those stay sparse.

**To specialize this**, send: which stations fade your runs worst, and the
baseline-brick result (pace + cadence-recovery time). I'll then set real targets
and bias the bricks to your actual leak points. If the issue is really aerobic
fade *across the whole race* rather than the post-station hit, that's engine work
— route to `hyrox-running-development-plan`.
