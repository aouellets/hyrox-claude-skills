# Example 01 — Complete data (verdict b: race with revised expectations)

**Request:**

> "HYROX Singles in 6 days, it's my A-race for the season. Last 3 weeks were
> rough: work travel killed two key sessions, my sim two weeks ago was 4 minutes
> off my target pace, and I've been sleeping ~6 hours. No pain, no illness, just
> tired and behind. Should I race, and how?"

## Verdict

**(b) Race with revised expectations.**

## Why

- **No health red flags, no pain, no illness** — racing itself is safe (so this
  is not (e), and not (d)). Health is weighed first and it's clear.
- **Performance has been disrupted**, not your health: two missed key sessions, a
  sim 4 minutes off target, and a run of short sleep. That doesn't make racing
  unwise — it makes your *original goal* unrealistic for next week.
- It's your A-race, so racing is clearly worth doing; the right move is to protect
  the result by re-anchoring the goal, not chasing a number the last three weeks
  didn't support.

There's also a secondary **(c)** element — with 6 days left and accumulated
fatigue, the priority is freshness, not fitness. Don't try to "make up" the
missed work now.

## Coaching judgment, not medical clearance

This is a coaching call about preparedness and expectations. It is **not** medical
clearance. Nothing here suggests you need a clinician — but if pain or illness
appears before race day, that changes the verdict.

## Assumptions & confidence

- Assumed the "4 minutes off" sim was at honest race effort and the fatigue is
  lifestyle/travel, not an unrecognized illness. **Confidence: medium-high.**

## What would change it

- If sleep and freshness rebound strongly in the next 4–5 days and you feel sharp
  on race morning → you can drift back toward **(a)** and your original pace band.
- If any symptom (sore throat, fever, a niggle turning sharp) appears → reassess;
  it could move to **(d)** or **(e)**.

## Next step / routing

- Re-anchor the goal and splits: `hyrox-goal-time-and-race-predictor` →
  `hyrox-race-pacing-planner` (plan to even *effort*, expect a slightly slower
  band than your A-goal).
- Lock in the final-week freshness: `hyrox-race-week-and-taper-planner`.
